  • Master Algorithm
  • Pedro Domingo
  • A qualitative and wordy book targeted to laymen about the basics of machine learning. The author intends to introduce the reader to major ML methodologies from neural networks and genetic algoritms to Bayesian networks and reinforcement learning. Each algorithm is highly complex and technical, and I think impossible to describe without using mathematics and examples. A reader will gain neither insights nor working knowledge out of this book, but at least should have a basic wikipedia-level idea of what machine learning is and is not.
