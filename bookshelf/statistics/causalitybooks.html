  • A qualitative and nontechnical discussion on causality, a rarity and mission improbable. The problem is without being structured as a textbook with formal use of math and definitions, it is very difficult to compare and contrast between standard statistical inference and causal models. To understand how causality can be tested, one must have a solid foundation of statistics/econometrics first, so causality is a topic unsuitable for a layman-style book.
