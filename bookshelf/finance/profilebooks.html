  • Fed Up
  • Colin Lancaster
  • Personal diary of a global macro hedge fund manager at tier-1 firms, primarily focusing on his trading days during the 2020 pandemics; he emphasises how global central banks, especially the Fed, keep injecting money supply into the financial system to pop up assets prices, exacerbating wealth inequality. A bit verbose and sometimes irrelevant, but a no-nonsense candid peek into his work life, which is largely useless to us.
