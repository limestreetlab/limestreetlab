There are $3$ cards in a box.
Card $1$ has both sides red.
Card $2$ has both sides black.
Card $3$ has one side red and one side black.
A card randomly drawn shows one side is red.
What's the probability that the other side is black.
\begin{align} P(RB|R) &= \frac{P(RB \cap R)}{P(R)} \\ &= \frac{P(R|RB)P(RB)}{P(R|RB)P(RB) + P(R|BB)P(BB) + P(R|RR)P(RR)} \\ &= \frac{(0.50)(0.33)}{(0.5)(0.33) + (0)(0.33) + (1)(0.33)} \\ & = \frac{0.165}{0.165+0.33} = 0.33 \end{align}
