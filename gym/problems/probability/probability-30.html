A deck of 52 cards is shuffled. What's the probability that the top four cards are of
  1. different values?
  2. different suits?
a. $$ \left( \frac{52 \cdot 48 \cdot 44 \cdot 40}{52 \cdot 51 \cdot 50 \cdot 49} \right) \approx 0.68 $$ b. $$ \left( \frac{52 \cdot 39 \cdot 26 \cdot 13}{52 \cdot 51 \cdot 50 \cdot 49} \right) \approx 0.11 $$
