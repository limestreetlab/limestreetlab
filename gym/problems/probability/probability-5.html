An ordinary deck of 52 cards is dealt into 4 hands.
What's the probability of each hand having an ace?
Let $A_1, A_2, A_3, A_4$ represent each ace.
The probability of $A_1$ in any hands is $1$.
Having $A_1$ fixed in one hand, the probability of $A_2$ in any of the remaining $3$ hands is $\frac{39}{51}$.
Having $A_1, A_2$ fixed in two different hands, the probability of $A_3$ in any of the remaining $2$ hands is $\frac{26}{50}$.
Hence, $P(A_1, A_2, A_3, A_4 \text{ in different hands})$ is, $$ \frac{52}{52} \cdot \frac{39}{51} \cdot \frac{26}{50} \cdot \frac{13}{49} \approx 0.105. $$
