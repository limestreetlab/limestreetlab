Mr. Musk plans to do a 500-km trip on an electric scooter.
The battery life is exponentially distributed with an average of 1000-km.
What's the probability that Mr. Musk can complete the trip?
Recall for exponential distribution, $$ f_X(x) = \lambda e^{-\lambda x} \quad \text{for } x\geq 0 , \qquad F_X(x) = 1-e^{-\lambda x} \quad \text{for } x\geq 0$$ The exponential rate is $\lambda = \frac{1}{E(X)} = \frac{1}{1000}.$
Hence, \begin{align} P(\text{life} \gt 500) & = 1 - P(\text{life} \leq 500) \\ & = 1 - (1 - e^{-\frac{500}{1000}}) \\ &= e^{-\frac{1}{2}}\\ & \approx 0.6 \end{align}
