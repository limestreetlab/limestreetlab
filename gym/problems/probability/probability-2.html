$N$ people in a meeting room throw their hats on the floor, which are then mixed.
Each person randomly picks one as he leaves.
What's the expected number of matches? That is, find the average number of people picking their own hats.
Let $X$ denote the number of matches, $$X = X_1 + X_2 + X_3 + \ldots + X_N$$ where $$X_i = \begin{cases} 1 \quad \text{if $i$th person picks own hat} \\ 0 \quad \text{otherwise} \end{cases} $$ We know, $$ E(X_i) = P(X_i = 1) = \frac{1}{N} $$ Because expected value of a sum is sum of the individual expected values, $$ E(X) = \underbrace{ E(X_1) + E(X_2) + \ldots + E(X_N) }_\text{N} = N \frac{1}{N} = 1 $$
