Check whether $\sum_{n=1}^\infty \frac{n}{n^3}$ converges.
Let's use the ratio test. \begin{align} \lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{\text{term}_{n+1}}{\text{term}_n} & = \lim_{n \to \infty}\frac{\frac{n+1}{3^{n+1}}}{\frac{n}{3^n}} \\ & = \lim_{n \to \infty}\frac{n+1}{3^{n+1}}\frac{3^n}{n} \\ & = \frac{1}{1} \cdot \frac{1}{3} < 1 \end{align} The limit is $ < 1$, so the series converges.
